Comprehensive Breast Surgery Expertise

Breast surgery is a core focus of A/Prof. Dean White’s practice. Whether patients seek functional improvement, aesthetic enhancement, or reconstruction following medical treatment, procedures are carefully tailored to individual anatomy and goals.

  • Breast Reduction: Relieves discomfort caused by heavy breasts, improving posture and daily comfort.
  • Breast Lift (Mastopexy): Restores shape and firmness affected by ageing, pregnancy, or weight changes.
  • Breast Augmentation: Enhances volume and contour while maintaining natural proportion.
  • Breast Reconstruction: Rebuilds form after mastectomy with compassionate, individualised planning.
  • Breast Implant Removal: Addresses implant concerns safely and carefully.
  • BIA-ALCL Management: Specialist assessment and treatment of breast implant associated ALCL.

Each consultation includes detailed discussion of risks, benefits, recovery expectations, and realistic outcomes so patients can make confident, informed decisions.
